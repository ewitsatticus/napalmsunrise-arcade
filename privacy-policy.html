Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2026

Napalm Sunrise is an independent publication. This page explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what we do not do with it.

What we collect

If you subscribe to the newsletter, we collect your email address. That is the entire data set we are interested in. We use it to send you new posts.

If you submit a question through the contact form, we collect the information you enter into that form: name (optional), email, subject, and message body.

Like most websites, our hosting platform automatically logs basic technical information when you visit: your IP address, browser type, the page you requested, and the time of the request. This information is used for security and reliability monitoring.

What we do not collect

We do not run third-party ad networks. We do not run third-party tracking pixels for advertising purposes. We do not sell your information to anyone, ever, under any circumstances.

How we use what we collect

Email addresses are used to deliver new posts and, occasionally, important publication updates. Contact form submissions are read by the editor and used to respond to your message or, in the case of Gorthothalog questions, considered for publication in the column.

If your question is selected for the Ask Gorthothalog column, it will be published. Names are removed unless you specifically request otherwise. Identifying details may be lightly altered to protect your privacy.

Cookies

The site uses minimal cookies, primarily for functional purposes (remembering that you have already entered the site, for example). It does not use third-party advertising cookies.

Your rights

You can unsubscribe from the newsletter at any time using the link at the bottom of any email. You can request that we delete your information by sending a message through the contact form. We will honor that request within a reasonable timeframe.

Third parties

The site is hosted on Shopify, which processes basic technical information as part of operating the platform. Newsletter delivery is also handled through Shopify's customer system. Shopify has its own privacy policy that governs that processing.

Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Material changes will be noted in a published post.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent through the contact form.
