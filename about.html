About Napalm Sunrise

What this is

Napalm Sunrise is an independent publication founded by Atticus Eby. It covers music, absurdist culture, and the kinds of questions most publications are too reasonable to take seriously.

The editorial approach is straightforward: pick a subject, commit to it fully, and follow the logic wherever it leads. Sometimes that logic leads to a rigorous survival analysis of the Wu-Tang Clan on the Oregon Trail. Sometimes it leads to a papal ranking system based on historical evidence. The methodology is consistent even when the subject matter is not.

The publication also houses Ask Gorthothalog, an advice column written by an 847-year-old orc whose credentials include several centuries of conflict resolution, three failed marriages, and the successful governance of a mid-sized territorial holding.

New content publishes weekly.
