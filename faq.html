FAQ

Common questions about Napalm Sunrise. If yours is not answered here, the contact form is at the bottom of the page.

What is Napalm Sunrise?

An independent publication founded by Atticus Eby. Editorial features, music coverage, and an advice column written by an 847-year-old orc named Gorthog. New posts publish weekly.

How often do you publish?

Weekly. Sometimes more. Never less.

Who writes for Napalm Sunrise?

Two regular contributors: Atticus Eby (editorial features, music coverage) and Gorthog (advice column). Both pages list their full archives.

Can I submit a question to Gorthog?

Yes. Use the contact form. Anonymous submissions are accepted. Selected questions are answered in the column. Gorthog reads everything; he answers what he answers.

Do you accept pitches from outside writers?

Not at the moment. The roster is intentionally small.

How do I subscribe?

Enter your email in any signup form on the site. We send new posts. We do not sell your information. We do not send promotional content.

Do you run advertising?

No banner ads. No sponsored content. No affiliate links.

Is Gorthog a real orc?

Define real.

Still have questions?

Use the contact form. Or subscribe and the answers will probably arrive eventually.
