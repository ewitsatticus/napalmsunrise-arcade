Gorthothalog

Gorthothalog is an 847-year-old orc and the resident advice columnist for Napalm Sunrise. He has been dispensing direct, unsentimental guidance since the fall of the Second Orcish Confederacy, which predates most of your cultural reference points by several hundred years.

His areas of expertise include career transitions, interpersonal conflict, the quiet desperation of modern existence, and the proper use of a two-handed axe in a meeting context. His methods are direct. His advice is practical. His patience for follow-up questions is limited.
